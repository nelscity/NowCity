Worst-Case Sea Level Rise Elevation Calculator (Boston, MA Area)

Estimate how much higher infrastructure might need to be built today to stay above projected mean sea level for its intended lifespan, based on a high-end ("worst-case" for planning) scenario.

Note: This calculator uses the NOAA 'High' scenario median projections from the 2022 Sea Level Rise Technical Report specifically for Boston, MA (relative to a 2000 baseline) as a plausible "worst-case" planning scenario. It calculates the projected rise from 2025 to the end of the lifespan.

This tool is for informational purposes only. Real-world projects require detailed, site-specific engineering assessments, consideration of local guidance, storm surge, tides, wave action, and appropriate safety factors.
